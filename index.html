about us

Empowering Your Water Solutions!

Aqua Blue Sales & Services is a leading force in Total Water Management, founded in 2015 under the guidance of Mr. Sandeep Yeole. Specializing in water and wastewater solutions, our dedicated team of experts offers a comprehensive range of services, from designing and supplying Water Treatment Plants to the operation and maintenance of critical infrastructure.

As authorized distributors of NCH CHEMAQUA Pvt Ltd, we bring cutting-edge solutions to water-related challenges, offering a diverse product range, including specialty chemicals for stress-tolerant performance. With 25 years of industry experience, Mr. Sandeep Yeole leads a team committed to delivering quality, service, and value, ensuring optimal resource use, cost reductions, and environmental sustainability.

Aqua Blue Sales & Services stands as a reliable partner for all water treatment needs, providing solutions that are efficient, sustainable, and tailored to industry demands.
